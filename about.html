About Me

I'm Minh Nhat Tran, or Trần Nhật Minh as it is known in Vietnamese. Nowadays I usually go by Minh Tran and Minh for short.

Born and raised in Hanoi, Vietnam, I got my Computer Science bachelor degree with a minor in Psychology at University of Minnesota, Twin-Cities (Go Gophers!). I am currently living in the Netherlands as I complete my Master degree in Human-Computer Interaction at Utrecht University.

My primary professional interests are in User Experience research and design, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Analytics/Visualization. More specifically, I am passionate about user centric design: ensuring intuitive and accessible interaction between people and machines, utilizing data-driven insights while making such data easy to understand in the first place, and advocating for explainable and ethical AI usage.

On a more personal note, my hobbies include reading (usualy novels, comics, and historical non-fiction), and playing video games (mostly single-player ones) to name a few. I recommend the Mistborn series by Brandon Sanderson if you want to get into fantasy novels, and Disco Elysium for those who want a novel disguised as a cRPG.
